Inspired by the POMO movement, this poster is meant to humorously comment on the consequences of exposing children to social media from too young of an age. The figures in the phone and tablet are his virtual parents, which replicates the adults who would rather distract their children with technology rather than take the time to raise them personally. The baby is drawn with various cartoon references such as the smoke fuming from his ears and the classic Bugs Bunny gloved hands to portray the influence technology is having on the child; he is literally becoming part of the virtual world.

ABOUT

Caitlin is working towards a Graphic Design major, hoping to spruce up design and enfuse personality into its core like a Frankenstein creation gone right. She's also on her way to conquering minors in Marketing, Psychology, and the Logos honors program at Grand View University to broaden her perspective and gain new inspiration. Life isn't all art though! Aside from creating, she enjoys mashed potatoes and dressing up her dog in dinosaur onesies. And yes, the pup loves both of those too.
